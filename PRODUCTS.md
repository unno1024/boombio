## 🛒 How to Order

1. Browse product catalog (if some products cannot be found, please email us for inquiry): global.kpbioie.com (view detailed information of biochemical reagents, lithium battery materials, lab consumables, etc.)

2. <b>Send inquiry(email us)</b>: Provide complete information including product name, CAS number, required quantity, destination, purity requirements and execution standards (e.g., pharmacopoeia standard, industrial standard, custom purity) (detailed information helps us confirm the program quickly)

3. Get professional quote & related details: We will feedback the product quote, procurement lead time (different products have different lead times, general reagents are 3-7 working days, special/custom products are 15-30 working days), and explain the relevant requirements of customs declaration, commodity inspection, and transportation identification report

4. Confirm order & global delivery: After you confirm the quote and related terms, we will start the procurement and distribution process, and provide full-process services including:
                  Customs declaration: Professional team handles import and export customs declaration procedures, sorts out all required documents to ensure smooth customs clearance, and avoids delays caused by incorrect document filling

5. Commodity inspection: According to the type of goods and the regulations of the destination country/region, we will confirm whether commodity inspection is required (dangerous goods, special biochemical reagents must go through commodity inspection procedures), and complete the relevant inspection and certification work

6. Transportation identification report: For goods such as lithium battery materials, flammable and explosive reagents, we will apply for a transportation identification report from a qualified institution (with CNAS/CMA certification) to ensure compliant transportation, and the handling cycle is 3-7 working days

7. Customs clearance: Provide one-stop customs clearance services for the destination country/region, assist in handling tax payment, inspection and other procedures, and ensure that the goods are smoothly released and delivered to the designated location, with a general customs clearance cycle of 1-3 working days (extended to 3-7 working days if inspected)
